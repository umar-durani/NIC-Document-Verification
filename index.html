IBCC VERIFICATION

Government of Pakistan has authorized IBCC to attest Certificates /Diplomas at secondary level and Higher secondary / intermediate levels awarded by all boards of Pakistan
Overall, IBCC plays a crucial role in maintaining educational standards, facilitating educational mobility, and ensuring the reliability and validity of academic qualifications within Pakistan and internationally.

HEC VERIFICATION

The Higher Education Commission of Pakistan (HEC) is an independent, autonomous, and constitutionally established institution of primary funding that oversees higher education in Pakistan ¹. Some of the key functions of HEC include ²: 

                  - Degree attestation
                  - Degree equivalence
                  - University information
                  - Research and development initiatives
                  - Accreditation
